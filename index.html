Privacy Policy for AI Recipe Finder

Last updated: November 25, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to AI Recipe Finder ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Ingredients: Text or voice input of ingredients you want to use
  • Photos: Images you upload for ingredient recognition (processed locally, not stored)
  • Saved Recipes: Recipes you save as favorites
  • Preferences: Dietary restrictions, cuisine preferences, serving sizes

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device ID: Anonymous device identifier for subscription verification and usage tracking
  • Usage Data: Number of recipes generated, features used
  • Crash Reports: Technical data when the app crashes (via Firebase Crashlytics)
  • Ad Interaction: Anonymous data about ad views (free tier only)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Generating personalized recipes using AI
  • Syncing your data across devices (optional)
  • Verifying and managing premium subscriptions
  • Tracking daily usage limits (free tier)
  • Improving app performance and fixing bugs
  • Showing relevant advertisements (free tier only)

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

Most of your data is stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences and local database. This includes:

  • Saved recipes and favorites
  • User preferences
  • Usage counts

4.2 Cloud Storage (Optional)

We use Firebase Firestore for optional cloud backup. This allows:

  • Data sync across multiple devices
  • Data recovery if you clear app data or reinstall
  • Subscription status verification

4.3 Security Measures

  • All data transmitted is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
  • Firebase Security Rules protect your cloud data
  • We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

5.1 Google AdMob (Free Tier Only)

  • Purpose: Display advertisements
  • Data Collected: Anonymous advertising ID, ad interaction data
  • Privacy Policy: Google Privacy Policy

5.2 Firebase (Google)

  • Purpose: Cloud storage, crash reporting, analytics
  • Data Collected: Device ID, app usage, crash logs
  • Privacy Policy: Firebase Privacy Policy

5.3 Google Gemini AI

  • Purpose: Generate recipe content
  • Data Sent: Ingredient list, preferences (not stored by Google)
  • Privacy Policy: Google Privacy Policy

5.4 Google Play In-App Billing

  • Purpose: Process subscription payments
  • Data Collected: Purchase history (managed by Google Play)
  • Privacy Policy: Google Payments Privacy Notice

6. Data Retention

  • Local Data: Stored until you delete the app or clear app data
  • Cloud Data: Stored indefinitely unless you request deletion
  • Usage Logs: Reset daily (recipe counts, ad views)

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access Your Data: View all data stored locally in the app
  • Delete Your Data: Clear app data or uninstall the app
  • Opt-Out of Cloud Sync: Use the app without Firebase (local only)
  • Request Data Export: Contact us for a copy of your cloud data
  • Opt-Out of Ads: Upgrade to Premium for ad-free experience

8. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and stored on servers located outside your country. We use Firebase (Google Cloud) which operates globally. By using our app, you consent to these transfers.
